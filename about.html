ABOUT Nayab



"Whoever said Disney was the happiest place on earth has never been to an antique store"


'NAYAB' is an Indian originated antiquity store located in Chandigarh. It is traditionally and culturally adorned store with a variety of Indian antiques. For example: miniatures, painting, sculptures, coins, clocks, vase, ornaments etc. New stuff doesn't usually have the character we adore, the chipped paint, the rust and the history. Every antique in our store has a significant history associated with it.
